Informational Writing: Topic Sentence & Conclusion

1. Which sentence is the BEST topic sentence?
Choose the sentence that tells the main idea for the whole text.
2. Which short paragraph has the BEST topic sentence?
Read A–C. Choose the paragraph where the FIRST sentence clearly tells the main idea.

Paragraph A
Ancient coins are made of metal. Some are gold and some are silver. People used them to buy things long ago.

Paragraph B
Ancient coins help people learn important things about the past. Many coins show leaders’ faces and symbols. These details tell us about history and power.

Paragraph C
Some coins have writing on them. Coins can be small or large. Coins can also be round or square.

3. What type of concluding sentence is this?
“People will continue to learn even more about the past from fossils.”
