Understanding Informational Writing

1. Which sentence is the topic sentence?

A fossil is a part of a plant or animal that has been preserved in the ground.

Fossils help people learn important things about the past.

Paleontologists study fossils such as bones and footprints.

2. What type of concluding sentence is this?

"People will continue to learn even more about the past from fossils."

3. What type of concluding sentence is this?

"I think ancient coins are important because they teach us about history."
