Informational Writing: Plan Before You Write

1. Which sentence is the BEST topic sentence?

A. Coins were used many years ago.

B. Ancient coins are made of metal.

C. Ancient coins help people learn important things about the past.

2. What type of concluding sentence is this?

"People will continue to discover new information about the past by studying coins."

3. What type of concluding sentence is this?

"I think archaeologists do important work because they help us understand history."

4. Planning with a KWL Chart

You are going to write about an object or job from the past.

K – What do you already know?

W – What do you want to know?

L – What might you learn?
